About Altitude Coffee

Welcome to Altitude Coffee, where exceptional quality meets authentic cafe culture in the heart of Dubai. We are passionate about serving the finest specialty coffee, crafted with precision and care by our expert baristas.

Altitude DXB is a specialty coffee shop in multiple locations across Dubai, designed for a community of like-minded individuals who seek calmness with a bit of adventure and a upbeat lifestyle. We serve more than coffee, we create moments of connection and creativity between the bean and the buyer.

Located in two convenient locations – The RIT Dubai Campus and the vibrant Al Quoz district – Altitude Coffee has become a beloved destination for coffee enthusiasts and students seeking that perfect cup.
